Simple Omelette recipe

An easy and quick dish,parfect for any meal.This classic Omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, oftionally filled with your choice of chesse, vagetables, or meats

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes

  • Preparation: 5 minutes

  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to teste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or all
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vagetables, cooked meats,hers

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl,beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed.You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffer texture.
  2. Heat the pan : Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil
  3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add fillings (optional) :Whene the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle. sprinkle your chosen fillings over one helf of the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve: As the omelette contiunes to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, then silde it anto a plate.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
